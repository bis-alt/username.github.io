Engine

  • The engine is the ‘heart’ of an automobile.


  • It is a complex machine designed to convert one or more forms of energy into mechanical energy that turns the road wheels1.


  • Car engines are built around a set of “cooking pots” called cylinders (usually anything from two to twelve of them, but typically four, six, or eight) inside which the fuel burns.


  • A car engine is an internal combustion engine — combustion takes place internally3.


  • There are different kinds of internal combustion engines, such as diesel engines and gas turbine engines3.
